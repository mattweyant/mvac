The Veteran must meet the criteria set forth by the Monument Committee in order to appear on the finalized monument(s). The criteria that were prescribed are as follows:

  1. Veteran, who served during one or more of the following:
    • World War II - Dec 7, 1941 through Dec 31, 1946
    • Korea - June 27, 1950 through Jan 31, 1955
    • Vietnam – Aug 5, 1964 through May 7, 1975 (Feb 28, 1961 for veterans who served “in country” before Aug 5 ,1964)
  2. Veteran must have entered have been discharged honorably (verifiable by military discharge/DD214)
  3. Must meet and supply proof of Medford residence requirements (in the form of certificate from Voter Registration Office). Veteran must meet 35 years Medford residency for WW II and Korean, and 20 years Medford residency for Vietnam Veterans.

All requests related to the Monument(s) must be made in writing, via the specific form. Each name change or addition requested requires a separate form to be complete.

Submitting a request form for inclusion only guarantees review by Monument Committee, and does not guarantee or imply that a particular Veterans name will appear on the finalized monument. Incomplete request forms will not be accepted.

The form must be to be completed in its entirety, signed, and mailed to:

City of Medford

Medford, MA 02155
